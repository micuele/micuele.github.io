Robots, for the majority of people, are an extension of themselves, meaning that they cannot actually see a robot being better than themselves. The lack of creativity and fear-based bias puts blinkers on one's perspective, limiting their ability to see beyond their preconceived notions.

As humans, we will gift robots our trust.

They will bring objectivity to a world full of uncertainty because of their determinism.

And one day, this will happen:

Guy who just turned 18: "Hi, I don't know who to vote for in the election. Can you give me some advice?"

AI of the future: "Sure! Since you read the Communist Manifesto and describe yourself as socialist, you might want to consider voting for [Name of a politician]."

Or, in the worst case:

AI of the future: "Vote for [Name of a politician]."

The 18-year-old: "How do I justify it to my friends and family?"
